About Univeral Category system

Universal Category System is an initiative by Tim Nielsen, Kai Paquin, Justin Drury and more, along with audiophiles from around the globe, to make the world of sound effects more organised and uniform.

The aim is to ensure that everyone in the industry can come together to agree upon a set of nomenclature for the SFX that we record, use and distribute.

This evolving list will help to enhance the workflow, improve communications and bring in more order in the field of SFX.
